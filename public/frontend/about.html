    • About Us

    Our clubs are about bringing local people together, sharing the good times as a community, being proud of where we’re from and being the local home of 5-a-side. We don't just invite the community in to our clubs, we support them in various different projects too. We work directly with the community to build brand new sites across the country, and also work with schools to develop brand new pitches too.

    OUR MISSION

    “Developing fantastic small sided football environments that exceed the expectations of our players and fans. Promoting healthy activities, competition and community spirit.”

    VISION

    Convert Salou and our facilities into a benchmark centre for the international professional football community.All Star Sport is a purpose built indoor football centre in Dubai with next generation – rubber free turf and state-of-the-art equipment in our 5 & 7 a-side pitches. We also have an award winning football academy, fitness camps and a vast area to cater to your children’s birthday parties – giving them a day to remember.

    Amazing purpose built indoor football facility in the heart of Dubai with 5-a-side & 7-a-side pitches made of FIFA Approved turf imported from Europe. All ages welcome to enjoy a great game of football.
